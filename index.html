Biography:

    Geng Sun (S'17-M'18) received the B.S. degree in communication engineering from Dalian Polytechnic University, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Jilin University, in 2011 and 2018, respectively. He was a Visiting Researcher with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, USA. He is a Professor in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Jilin University, and also a Senior Research Scholar in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (Collaborative Supervisor: Prof. Dusit Niyato). His research interests include UAV Communications and Networking, 6G Intelligent Reflecting Surface Communication, Mobile Edge Computing, Internet of Things (IoT) Collaborative Communication and Sensing, Generative Artificial Intelligence, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Evolutionary Learning, and Multi-objective Optimization.

    He published more than 100 papers including IEEE/ACM ToN, IEEE JSAC, IEEE TMC, IEEE TWC, IEEE TCOM, IEEE TAP, IEEE TAP, IEEE IoT-J, IEEE TIV, IEEE TIM, IEEE CMOST, IEEE WCM, IEEE CL, ACM TOSN, ACM TCPS, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE GLOBECOM, IEEE ICC, and IEEE WCNC, which have received more than 1800 Google citations.
